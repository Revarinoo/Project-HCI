NEW GAME MODE

QUARANTINE

In this game mode, the Rainbow team must complete a mission to a laboratory that has long been abandoned. This laboratory was abandoned because it was caught carrying out illegal experiments on humans. The Rainbow Team is required to look for evidence of the result of illegal experiments that have been carried out there.

UNKNOWN CREATURE

As long as the Rainbow Team carries out its mission in the laboratory, they will be accompanied by an unknown creature. This creature is said to have originated from a virus infection from the results of illegal experiments that have been tested on humans.

INFECTION



The Rainbow Team must be careful during their mission in the laboratory. Because the creatures that are there can infect you with a virus that has mutated so that the Rainbow Team can become infected and become one of them or die.

CURE



While in this mode, each member of the Rainbow Team gets a bracelet that serves to cure the effects of a viral infection given by unknown creature. This bracelet only works once in each match. This bracelet can also be an indicator of whether you are infected or not.
